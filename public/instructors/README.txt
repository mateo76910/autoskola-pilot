Instructor photos
=================

Put instructor photos in this folder. They are served from the
/instructors/ URL path at runtime.

Expected files (referenced by src/components/sections/Instructors.tsx):

  - davor.jpg
  - sara.jpg
  - marko.jpg
  - elena.jpg

Recommendations:
  - Square or portrait orientation (the card crop is ~1:1).
  - At least 600×600 px for crisp display on retina screens.
  - Use .jpg for photos, .webp if you want smaller files.
  - If you rename a file, update the `image` field for that
    instructor in src/components/sections/Instructors.tsx.
